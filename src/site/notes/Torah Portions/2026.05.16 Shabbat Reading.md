# [Exo 19:6b-20:26](https://www.biblegateway.com/passage/?search=Exo%2019%3A6b-20%3A26&version=WEB;NET;OJB)	
- Moses went up (1) to Yah on [[Gate of Wisdom/Dwelling Places/Realm of Humans/Mount Sinai\|Mount Sinai]].
	- Yah said
		- you have seen what I did to the Egyptians
		- I bore you on eagles wings and brought you TO myself
		- THEREFORE
			- you will indeed obey my voice
			- keep my covenant
		- THEN
			- you will be my own possession
				- from among all the people
					- for all the earth is mine
			- you will be to me a kingdom of priests <mark style="background: #CD04BBA6;">//all will be living set apart and will have access to Yah//</mark>
			- a [[Gate of Wisdom/H/Holy\|Holy]]/set apart nation  <mark style="background: #CD04BBA6;">//separate and different than others//</mark>
		- These are the words which you speak to the children of [[Gate of Wisdom/Beings/Human Beings/Israel\|Israel]]
- Moses came down - called the Elders 
	- set before them all "these words"
		- which YHWH commanded
			- commanded = ṣiw-wā-hū (צִוָּהוּ) - which He [YHWH] had commanded him; This root (צוה) is the standard Hebrew verb for divine commands throughout the Torah
	- ALL the people answered together
		- All that YHWH has spoken we will do
- Moses went up (2) to Yah on Mt. Sinai
	- reported the words of the people
	- Yah said
		- I come to you in a thick cloud <mark style="background: #CD04BBA6;">//makes me think of Revelation 1 - coming on the clouds//</mark>
			- that the people 
				- may hear when I speak with you
				- and believe you forever
	- Moses told the words of the people to YHWH
	- Yah said
		- go to the people
			- sanctify them today and tomorrow
			- let them wash their garments
			- and be ready for the third day <mark style="background: #CD04BBA6;">//makes me think of Jesus - rose on the third day and went to the people//</mark>
				- on the third day YHWH will come down
					- in the sight of ALL the people 
					- set bounds
						- don't go up onto the mountain
						- don't touch its border
							- who ever touches 
								- surely put to death <mark style="background: #CD04BBA6;">//makes me think of Genesis with Adam/Eve don't eat//</mark>
								- stoned or shot through
									-animal or man
								- shall not live
						- when the [[Zunpublished/Trumpets\|Trumpets]] <mark style="background: #CD04BBA6;">//shofar//</mark> sounds
							- they shall come up to the mountain <mark style="background: #CD04BBA6;">//permissive imperfect, “they may go up.” - so after the sound, they were permitted to ascend the mountain.//</mark>
- Moses went down to the people
	- sanctified the people
	- they washed their cloths
	- Be ready on the third day
	- don't have sexual relations with a woman
- THIRD DAY in morning
	- thunders and lightnings <mark style="background: #CD04BBA6;">//thunder = qōl (קוֹל) voices//</mark>
	- thick cloud on the mountain <mark style="background: #CD04BBA6;">//makes me think of Acts 2//</mark>
	- loud trumpet
	- ALL the people who were in the camp trembled
	- Moses lead the people out of the camp to meet Yah
		- they stood at the lower part of the mountain
	- All Mt Sinai smoked
		- because YHHW descended on it in fire
		- smoke ascended 
		- the mountain quaked greatly
	- the trumpet grew louder and louder <mark style="background: #CD04BBA6;">//the voice was going and becoming very strong//</mark>
		- <mark style="background: #CD04BBA6;">Note: in Acts 2:6 Now when this sound = phōnē (Φωνή) voice, sound or noise</mark>
	- Moses spoke and Yah answered him by a voice
	- YHWH came down on Mt. Sinia
		- to the top of the mountain
		- called Moses to the top of the mountain
		- Moses went up (3)
			- Yah said
				- Go down
				- warn the people
					- lest they break through to YHWH to gaze and many perish
					- let the priest
						- who come near to YHWH
						- sanctify themselves
							- lest YHWH break out on them <mark style="background: #CD04BBA6;">//work destruction//</mark>
			- Moses said
				- The people can't come up to Mt. Sinai
					- because you warned us - set bounds - sanctified it <mark style="background: #CD04BBA6;">//interesting how Moses doesn't even conceive of going against what Yah has told him//</mark>
			- Yah said
				- Go down
				- Bring Aaron with you
				- don't let the priests and the people break through to come up to YHWH
- Moses went down to the people
	- Gave the [[Gate of Wisdom/T/Ten Words\|Ten Words]] //[[Gate of Wisdom/T/Testimony\|Testimony]], [[Gate of Wisdom/Dwelling Places/Tabernacle/Most Set Apart Place/The Witness\|The Witness]]//
		- No gods before YHWH
		- No [[Gate of Wisdom/I/idol\|idol]], nor [[Gate of Wisdom/I/image\|image]] of anything
			- do not bow to them or serve them
			- YHWH is a jealous [[Gate of Wisdom/Beings/Divine Council/Elohim\|Elohim]] <mark style="background: #CD04BBA6;">//jealous = zeal//</mark>
				- iniquity visited to generations of those who hate me
					- //<mark style="background: #CD04BBA6;">generations: The Hebrew word for “generation” (דּוֹר / dôr) does not appear in verses 5 or 6. The word “generation” is implied from the context of fathers and children, but it is not explicitly stated</mark>.//
					- //<mark style="background: #CD04BBA6;">hate - the word implies the idea of defiantly rejecting and opposing Yah's word.</mark>//
				- loving kindness to those who 
					- love me
					- keep my [[Gate of Wisdom/Right Standing/Commands (Mitzvot)\|Commands (Mitzvot)]]
		- Do not misuse [[Gate of Wisdom/N/Name\|Name]]
		- Remember the [[Gate of Wisdom/Appointed Time/Sabbath\|Sabbath]] Day
			- keep it [[Gate of Wisdom/H/Holy\|Holy]]/set apart
				- six days of labor
				- seventh is a day of sabbath to YHWH
					- no one is to do work
			- YHWH blessed the Sabbath day and set it apart
		- Honor father and mother
			- days may be long in the land which Yah gives you
		- Do not commit murder <mark style="background: #CD04BBA6;">//ratsakh (רָצַח) any unauthorized killing//</mark>
		- Do not commit adultery
		- Do not steal
		- Do not give false testimony <mark style="background: #CD04BBA6;">//ʿed shaqer (עֵד שָׁקֶר) a lying witness//</mark>
		- Do not covert your neighbor's <mark style="background: #CD04BBA6;">//khamad (חָמַד) greedy desire - used in the Garden of Eden - the tree was khamad//</mark>
			- house
			- wife
			- servants
			- animals
			- or anything
	- All the people 
		- perceived the thunderings, lightnings, sound of trumpets, mountain smoke
		- they trembled and stayed at a distance
		- they told Moses
			- speak with us yourself
			- we will listen
			- but don't let god speak with us
				- lest we die <mark style="background: #CD04BBA6;">//makes me think about how the serpent twisted what Yah said//</mark>
		- Moses said
			- don't be afraid
				- god has come 
					- to test/prove you
					- that his fear be be before you
					- that you won't sin
			- the people stayed at a distance
			- Moses came near to the thick darkness <mark style="background: #CD04BBA6;">//makes me think of the darkness in the beginning - darkness normally represent chaos//</mark>
				- where god was
				- Yah said tell the children of Israel
					- you have seen I have talked with you from [[Gate of Wisdom/Dwelling Places/Realm of Yah/Heaven\|Heaven]]
						- do not make gods of silver
						- do not make gods of gold
						- make an altar **of earth** for me
							- sacrifice on it your
								- [[Gate of Wisdom/Offering/Burnt Offering\|Burnt Offering]]
								- [[Gate of Wisdom/Offering/Peace Offering\|Peace Offering]]
							- in every place where i record my [[Gate of Wisdom/N/Name\|Name]]
								- I will come to you and bless you
							- altar of stone
								- not cut stones
								- if you lift your tool on it, you have polluted it
							- do not go up by steps to my altar
								- that your nakedness may not be exposed to it
									- nakedness = ervah (עֶרְוָה) private parts

<mark style="background: #CD04BBA6;">When Yah says he is a zealous god - iniquities and kindness makes me think of how later he says he lays out blessings and curses. If you choose to live by his ways, there are blessings. Those outside of his ways end up leading to curses, some being natural consequences.</mark>
