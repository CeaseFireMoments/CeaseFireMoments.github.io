# Psalm 122

[[_The Scrolls/3. Ketuvim (Writings)/19 - Psalm/Ps-121\|← Psalm 121]] | [[_The Scrolls/3. Ketuvim (Writings)/19 - Psalm/Psalm\|Psalm]] | [[_The Scrolls/3. Ketuvim (Writings)/19 - Psalm/Ps-123\|Psalm 123 →]]
***



###### v1 
I was glad when they said to me, "Let's go to Yahweh's house!" 

###### v2 
Our feet are standing within your gates, Jerusalem, 

###### v3 
Jerusalem, that is built as a city that is compact together, 

###### v4 
where the tribes go up, even Yah's tribes, according to an ordinance for Israel, to give thanks to Yahweh's name. 

###### v5 
For there are set thrones for judgment, the thrones of David's house. 

###### v6 
Pray for the peace of Jerusalem. Those who love you will prosper. 

###### v7 
Peace be within your walls, and prosperity within your palaces. 

###### v8 
For my brothers' and companions' sakes, I will now say, "Peace be within you." 

###### v9 
For the sake of the house of Yahweh our God, I will seek your good.
