# Psalm 128

[[_The Scrolls/3. Ketuvim (Writings)/19 - Psalm/Ps-127\|← Psalm 127]] | [[_The Scrolls/3. Ketuvim (Writings)/19 - Psalm/Psalm\|Psalm]] | [[_The Scrolls/3. Ketuvim (Writings)/19 - Psalm/Ps-129\|Psalm 129 →]]
***



###### v1 
Blessed is everyone who fears Yahweh, who walks in his ways. 

###### v2 
For you will eat the labor of your hands. You will be happy, and it will be well with you. 

###### v3 
Your wife will be as a fruitful vine in the innermost parts of your house, your children like olive shoots around your table. 

###### v4 
Behold, this is how the man who fears Yahweh is blessed. 

###### v5 
May Yahweh bless you out of Zion, and may you see the good of Jerusalem all the days of your life. 

###### v6 
Yes, may you see your children's children. Peace be upon Israel.
