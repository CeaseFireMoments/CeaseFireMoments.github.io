# Define
- Hebrew: bikkurim (בִּכּוּרִים) - firstfruits, first ripe produce  
- Greek: aparche (ἀπαρχή) - firstfruits, first portion  

# Related words
- Hebrew: re'shit (רֵאשִׁית) - beginning, first, chief  
- Greek: prototokos (πρωτότοκος) - firstborn  
- [[Gate of Wisdom/Appointed Time/Appointed Time\|Appointed Time]]

# Additional Info
- Leviticus 23:10, Numbers 28:26 (bikkurim)  
- Romans 8:23, 1 Corinthians 15:20, 15:23 (aparche)  

# Connections
- Romans 8:23 links firstfruits (aparche) of the Spirit to the revealing of the sons of God and redemption of the body.
