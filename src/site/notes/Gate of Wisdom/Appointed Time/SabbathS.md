# Define
- Hebrew: shabbəṯôtay (שַׁבְּתוֹתַי)  “cessation, rest” (plural: shabbatot)

# Additional Info
- The seventh-day Sabbath every week + the special annual Sabbaths (high holy days) on which all regular work ceases completely, regardless of the day of the week they fall.
	- **Weekly Sabbath** - Every 7th day (Friday sunset to Saturday sunset) [[_The Scrolls/1. Torah (Law of Moses)/02 - Exodus/Exod-20#v8\|Exod-20#v8]]-11; [[_The Scrolls/1. Torah (Law of Moses)/02 - Exodus/Exod-31#v14\|Exod-31#v14]]–17; [[_The Scrolls/1. Torah (Law of Moses)/02 - Exodus/Exod-35#v2\|Exod-35#v2]]–3; [[_The Scrolls/1. Torah (Law of Moses)/03 - Leviticus/Lev-23#v3\|Lev-23#v3]]
	- **1st Day of Unleavened Bread** - 15th day of the 1st month (Nisan 15) [[_The Scrolls/1. Torah (Law of Moses)/03 - Leviticus/Lev-23#v7\|Lev-23#v7]]; [[_The Scrolls/1. Torah (Law of Moses)/04 - Numbers/Num-28#v18\|Num-28#v18]]
	- **7th Day of Unleavened Bread** - 21st day of the 1st month (Nisan 21) [[_The Scrolls/1. Torah (Law of Moses)/03 - Leviticus/Lev-23#v8\|Lev-23#v8]]; [[_The Scrolls/1. Torah (Law of Moses)/04 - Numbers/Num-28#v25\|Num-28#v25]]
	- **Day of Pentecost (Feast of Weeks)** - 50 days after the wave-sheaf (Sivan 6 in most years) [[_The Scrolls/1. Torah (Law of Moses)/03 - Leviticus/Lev-23#v21\|Lev-23#v21]]; [[_The Scrolls/1. Torah (Law of Moses)/04 - Numbers/Num-28#v26\|Num-28#v26]]
	- **Feast of Trumpets (Rosh Hashanah)** - 1st day of the 7th month (Tishri 1) [[_The Scrolls/1. Torah (Law of Moses)/03 - Leviticus/Lev-23#v24\|Lev-23#v24]]–25; [[_The Scrolls/1. Torah (Law of Moses)/04 - Numbers/Num-29#v1\|Num-29#v1]]
	- **Day of Atonement (Yom Kippur)** - 10th day of the 7th month (Tishri 10) [[_The Scrolls/1. Torah (Law of Moses)/03 - Leviticus/Lev-16#v29\|Lev-16#v29]]–31; [[_The Scrolls/1. Torah (Law of Moses)/03 - Leviticus/Lev-23#v28\|Lev-23#v28]]–32; [[_The Scrolls/1. Torah (Law of Moses)/04 - Numbers/Num-29#v7\|Num-29#v7]]
	- **1st Day of Tabernacles (Sukkot)** - 15th day of the 7th month (Tishri 15) [[_The Scrolls/1. Torah (Law of Moses)/03 - Leviticus/Lev-23#v34\|Lev-23#v34]]–35; [[_The Scrolls/1. Torah (Law of Moses)/04 - Numbers/Num-29#v12\|Num-29#v12]]
	- **8th Day after Tabernacles (Shemini Atzeret)** - 22nd day of the 7th month (Tishri 22)
