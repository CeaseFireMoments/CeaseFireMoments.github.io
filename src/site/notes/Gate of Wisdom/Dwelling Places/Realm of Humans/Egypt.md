# Location: 
- **Biblical**: mitsrayim (מִצְרַיִם)
- **Modern Location**: Egypt

# Significance:
- **First mention**: Genesis 12:10 (Abraham sojourns there due to famine)
- **Last mention**: Revelation (Aigyptos / Αἴγυπτος, symbolically as Sodom and Egypt where the Lord was crucified, Revelation 11:8)
- **Symbolic**: Bondage, oppression, slavery to sin, the world, place of refuge that turns to tyranny
- **Major Events**:
  - Abraham's sojourn in Egypt (Genesis 12:10-20)
  - Joseph sold into Egypt, rises to power, brings family there (Genesis 37-50)
  - Israelites enslaved in Egypt (Exodus 1)
  - The ten plagues on Egypt (Exodus 7-12)
  - The Exodus and Red Sea crossing (Exodus 12-14)
  - Flight of the Holy Family to Egypt (Matthew 2:13-15)

# People
- Pharaoh (multiple, e.g., of Joseph's time, of Moses' time)
- Joseph (prominent in Egypt)
- Moses (raised in Egypt)
- Aaron
- Miriam
- Potiphar
- Pharaoh's daughter (who raised Moses)
