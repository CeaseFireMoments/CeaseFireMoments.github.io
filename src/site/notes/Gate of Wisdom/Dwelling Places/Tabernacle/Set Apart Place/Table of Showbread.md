# Define
- twelve loaves prepared weekly for the tabernacle and later, the temple.
	- See Exod 25:30; 35:13; 39:36; Lev 24:5-9.
- placed on a table in the holy place [[Zunpublished/Set Apart Place\|Set Apart Place]] of the [[Gate of Wisdom/Dwelling Places/Tabernacle/Tabernacle\|Tabernacle]]
	- on the north side opposite the lampstand
- each Sabbath the priest placed fresh bread on the table
	- the loaves from the previous week were then given to Aaron and his descendants, who ate them in the holy place
