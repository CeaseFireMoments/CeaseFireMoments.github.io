**One-Year FEMALE Reading Plan for All Applicable Biblical Commands**  
Starting March 20; 365 day

This plan uses **only the Bible** (Torah for OT commands + Gospels for Jesus). Every command, statute (chuqim), ordinance/charge (mishmerot/tsavah), judgment (mishpatim), precept (pikkudim), or general mitzvah that applies to all people is included.  

**Exclusions** :  
- Anything for priests/Kohanim only
- Anything requiring the Temple/sacrifices/altar 
- Strictly male-specific

**Classification** (Bible-based, no external tradition):  
- **[[Gate of Wisdom/Right Standing/Commands (Mitzvot)\|Commands (Mitzvot)]]**: General “you shall” or direct imperative.  
- **[[Gate of Wisdom/Right Standing/Statutes (Chuqim)\|Statutes (Chuqim)]]**: Decrees/statutes often without obvious reason (e.g., dietary, certain holy days).  
- **[[Zunpublished/Ordinance Mishmerot\|Ordinance Mishmerot]] / [[Gate of Wisdom/Right Standing/Charge (Tsavah)\|Charge (Tsavah)]])**: Observances, charges, or duties to keep/guard.  
- **[[Gate of Wisdom/Right Standing/Judgments (Mishpatim)\|Judgments (Mishpatim)]]**: Civil, moral, or case-law rulings (e.g., Exodus 21–23 section titled “mishpatim”).  
- **[[Gate of Wisdom/Right Standing/Precepts (Pikkudim)\|Precepts (Pikkudim)]]**: Precepts or careful instructions (as in Psalm 119 style for God’s directives).  

The plan assigns **one primary command per day** (verses chosen directly from the Bible). With ~270 applicable Torah commands + ~40 core Jesus commands = ~310 total, the first ~310 days introduce new ones in biblical order; remaining days are review/meditation on prior commands or related verses. All are covered by year’s end.
