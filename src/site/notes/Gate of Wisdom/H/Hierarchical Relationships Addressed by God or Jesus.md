Hierarchical Relationships Addressed by God or Jesus

God over all creation
* Genesis 1:1
* Job 38-41 — God speaks directly, establishing His authority over all created things
* Isaiah 45:9-12

God over mankind collectively
* Genesis 2:16-17
* Exodus 20:1-17
* Deuteronomy 5:1-22

God over nations
* Genesis 15:14 — God judges nations
* Exodus 9:16 — God raises up and brings down nations
* Daniel 4:17 — God rules over kingdoms of men (spoken by heavenly messenger per God's decree)

God over spiritual beings / angels
* Job 1:6-12 — God sets limits on Satan
* Isaiah 6:1-3 — seraphim subordinate before God

God the Father over Jesus the Son
* John 4:34 — Jesus does the will of the one who sent Him
* John 5:19
* John 5:30
* John 6:38
* John 14:28
* John 17:4

Jesus over the Church / disciples
* Matthew 28:18
* John 13:13-14
* John 15:14-15

Jesus over death and sin
* John 11:25
* Matthew 16:18

Older over younger
* Genesis 25:23 — God tells Rebekah the older shall serve the younger (inverting expected hierarchy)
* Leviticus 19:32 — rise before the aged

Parents over children
* Exodus 20:12
* Deuteronomy 5:16
* Proverbs 1:8
* Proverbs 13:24
* Proverbs 22:6
* Proverbs 23:13-14

Moses / appointed leaders over Israel
* Exodus 18:21-22
* Numbers 11:16-17 — God appoints seventy elders to share governing authority
* Deuteronomy 1:13-15

Priests / Levites over sacred order
* Numbers 3:10
* Numbers 18:1-7
* Leviticus 21 — distinct rules and authority for priests

Kings over people
* Deuteronomy 17:14-20 — God authorizes and defines limits of kingship
* 1 Samuel 8:7 — God reluctantly sanctions the king structure

Judges over people
* Exodus 18:21-26
* Deuteronomy 16:18-20

Masters over servants
* Exodus 21:1-11
* Leviticus 25:39-55

Mankind over creation / animals
* Genesis 1:26-28 — given jointly to male and female
* Genesis 9:2 — reaffirmed after the flood

Prophet as God's mouthpiece over people
* Deuteronomy 18:18-19 — God establishes the prophetic authority structure

Husband over wife
* Genesis 3:16 — consequence disobedience
