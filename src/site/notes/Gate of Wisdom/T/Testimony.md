# Define
- Hebrew:  ēdût (עֵדוּת)
	- testimony, witness (especially divine testimony or covenant witness)
	    - Exodus 31:18 – "[[Gate of Wisdom/Dwelling Places/Tabernacle/Most Set Apart Place/Tablets of Testimony\|Tablets of Testimony]]" (לֻחֹת הָעֵדֻת) (commonly referred to as [[Gate of Wisdom/T/Ten Words\|Ten Words]] or [[Gate of Wisdom/C/Ten Commandments\|Ten Commandments]])
	    - Psalm 119:88 – "according to your testimony" (כְּעֵדְוָתֶךָ)

- Greek: martyria (martyria) 
	- Witness, testimony (a declaration or report of what one has seen, heard, or knows)
    - Evidence given (especially in a legal or public sense)
    - The content of what is witnessed concerning Christ
# Related words
- ‘ed (עֵד) - witness  
- ‘edah (עֵדָה) - testimony, witness  
- ‘ud (עוּד) - to bear witness, testify (root)
# Additional Info
- Refers to the tablets of the law (covenant witness) and the place of their keeping (Ark of the Testimony) [[Gate of Wisdom/Dwelling Places/Tabernacle/Most Set Apart Place/Tables of Stone\|Tables of Stone]], [[Gate of Wisdom/Dwelling Places/Tabernacle/Most Set Apart Place/Tablets of Testimony\|Tablets of Testimony]]
# Connections
- Exodus 16:34 (before the Testimony)  
- Exodus 25:16, 21 (Testimony placed in the Ark)  
- Exodus 31:18 (tables of the Testimony)  
- Hebrews 9:4 (jar of manna with the Testimony)-
