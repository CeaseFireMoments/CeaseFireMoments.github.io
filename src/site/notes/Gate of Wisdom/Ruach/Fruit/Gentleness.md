# Define

Phil 4:4-23
- Hebrew: anav (עָנָו) - humble, meek, lowly, gentle (commonly linked to the concept of gentleness/meekness in biblical thought)	- 
- Greek: epieikēs (ἐπιεικής) - gentle, reasonable, moderate, forbearing, yielding	- 

Galations 5
	prautēs (πραΰτης) - gentleness, meekness, humility (frequent in NT, e.g. Gal 5:23)  

# Related words
- anavah (עֲנָוָה) - humility, meekness (OT equivalent concept)

# Additional Info
- epieikeia (ἐπιείκεια) - forbearance, clemency, gentleness (related noun form)
