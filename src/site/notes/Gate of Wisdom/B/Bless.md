# Define
- Hebrew: barak (בָּרַךְ) - to bless, to praise, to kneel 

- Greek: eulogeo (εὐλογέω) - to bless, to speak well of, to praise (equivalent)- 


# Related words
- barak (בָּרַךְ), berekah (בְּרָכָה) - [[Gate of Wisdom/Consequence/Obedience/Blessing\|Blessing]]
- yadah (יָדָה) - to praise, to give thanks
- hodah (הוֹדָה) - to thank, to confess
- eulogia (εὐλογία) - blessing, praise (equivalent)
