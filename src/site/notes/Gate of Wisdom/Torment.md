# Define
- Hebrew: 
	- innuy (עִנּוּי) affliction, oppression
	- yasar (יָסַר) – To chastise/discipline painfully
- No exact single term, but related to Greek: basanismos (βασανισμός) torment, torture, severe pain (often through testing or punishment). Torment imagery in Revelation echoes OT judgments like Sodom (fire/brimstone, Genesis 19).

# Related words


# Additional Info
In Revelation 14:10-11, the primary noun for "torment" is basanismos (βασανισμός), describing the ongoing torment of those who worship the beast.
