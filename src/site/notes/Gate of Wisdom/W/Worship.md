# Define
- **Hebrew**: 
	- shachah (שָׁחָה) (verb) - to bow down, prostrate oneself, do obeisance, do reverence (especially in an act of submission or adoration).
	- hishtachavah (הִשְׁתַּחֲוָה) - the reflexive/intensive form of shachah; to prostrate oneself, bow down in worship, do homage.

- **Greek**: proskyneō (προσκυνέω) - to prostrate oneself, do obeisance, bow down in reverence, worship, show homage (literally “to kiss toward”; used in NT for both physical bowing and the inward act of worship
