# Define
- Hebrew: dābār (דָּבָר)
	- word, thing, matter, message, command, decree
	- Especially used for the spoken word or revelation of God

- Greek: logos (λόγος)
	- Word, speech, message
    - Statement, declaration, or teaching
    - The expressed will or revelation of God (especially Scripture)
