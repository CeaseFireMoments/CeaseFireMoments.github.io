# Father's Inheritance (Human Father)
## Define
- The inheritance (Hebrew: nachalah) refers to the material and territorial possessions passed from a father to his children, often divided among sons, with the [[Zunpublished/Firstborn\|Firstborn]] receiving a double portion as part of the [[Gate of Wisdom/B/Birthright\|Birthright]]. In a broader sense, it could also include God’s covenant promises, such as the land of Canaan for Israel
