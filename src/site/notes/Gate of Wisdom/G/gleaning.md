# Define

- Hebrew: leqet (לֶקֶט) gleanings, what is gathered after reaping
- Greek: apopiptonta (ἀποπίπτοντα) things that fall off, fallen gleanings

# Related words

- leqet (לֶקֶט)
- peret (פֶּרֶט) fallen grapes (vineyard parallel)
- pe'ah (פֵּאָה) corner/edge of field

# Additional Info

- Leviticus 19:9 Hebrew: ...וְלֶקֶט קְצִירְךָ לֹא תְלַקֵּט... (veleqet qetsircha lo telakket)
