# Define
- Hebrew: yəšûʿāh (יְשׁוּעָה) - salvation, deliverance  
- Greek: sōtēria (σωτηρία) - salvation, deliverance  

# Related words
- Hebrew: yēšaʿ (יֵשַׁע) - salvation, deliverance  
- Hebrew: tsedeq (צֶדֶק) - [[Gate of Wisdom/Right Standing/Righteousness\|righteousness]]   

# Additional Info
- Frequently used for God’s concrete acts of deliverance from enemies, oppression, or danger (Exodus 14:13, 15:2; Psalm 3:8, 18:2, 118:14)
- Carries the idea of active intervention and victory rather than just forgiveness of sins
- Often paired with צֶדֶק (tsedeq – righteousness) as in Isaiah 61:10

# Connections
- Isaiah 61:10 connects garments of salvation with robe of righteousness  
- Linked thematically to Romans 8:30 (justified and glorified)
