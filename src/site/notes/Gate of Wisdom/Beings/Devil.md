# Define
- Greek: diabolos (διάβολος): Accuser, slanderer (one who attacks with false or malicious charges).
- Hebrew: satan (שָׂטָן): adversary, accuser (used interchangeably in function, as in Job 1:6–12; Zechariah 3:1–2).
