# Define
- Hebrew:
	- shaliaḥ (שָׁלִיחַ) — sent one, messenger, agent

- Greek:
	- apostolos (ἀπόστολος) — one sent, messenger, delegate


# Related words
- apostellō (ἀποστέλλω) — to send
- angelos (ἄγγελος) — [[Gate of Wisdom/Beings/Messenger\|Messenger]]

# Additional Info
- Apostles mentioned by name:
	- **Simon Peter** — Matthew 10:2; Mark 3:16; Luke 6:14; Acts 1:13
	- **Andrew** (brother of Peter) — Matthew 10:2; Mark 3:18; Luke 6:14; Acts 1:13
	- **James** (son of Zebedee) — Matthew 10:2; Mark 3:17; Luke 6:14; Acts 1:13
	- **John** (brother of James, son of Zebedee) — Matthew 10:2; Mark 3:17; Luke 6:14; Acts 1:13
	- **Philip** — Matthew 10:3; Mark 3:18; Luke 6:14; Acts 1:13
	- **Bartholomew** — Matthew 10:3; Mark 3:18; Luke 6:14; Acts 1:13
	- **Thomas** — Matthew 10:3; Mark 3:18; Luke 6:15; Acts 1:13
	- **Matthew** (the tax collector) — Matthew 10:3; Mark 3:18; Luke 6:15; Acts 1:13
	- **James** (son of Alphaeus) — Matthew 10:3; Mark 3:18; Luke 6:15; Acts 1:13
	- **Thaddaeus** (also Judas son of James or Jude) — Matthew 10:3; Mark 3:18; Luke 6:16; Acts 1:13
	- **Simon** (the Zealot) — Matthew 10:4; Mark 3:18; Luke 6:15; Acts 1:13
	- **Judas Iscariot** (replaced) — Matthew 10:4; Mark 3:19; Luke 6:16
	- **Matthias** (replacement for Judas) — Acts 1:26
	- **Jesus** — Hebrews 3:1 (the apostle and high priest)
	- **Paul** (also Saul) — Romans 1:1; 1 Corinthians 1:1; Galatians 1:1 (and many epistle openings)
	- **Barnabas** — Acts 14:4, 14
	- **James** (the Lord's brother) — Galatians 1:19 (and implied in 1 Corinthians 15:7)
	- **Andronicus** — Romans 16:7 (outstanding among the apostles)
	- **Junia** (or Junias) — Romans 16:7 (outstanding among the apostles; widely regarded as female in early tradition)
	- **Epaphroditus** — Philippians 2:25 (your apostle/messenger)
