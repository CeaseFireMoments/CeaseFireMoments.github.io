# Titles and Roles
- **Faithful and True** – Rev 19:11        
- **In righteousness judges and makes war** – Rev 19:11        
- **King of kings and Lord of lords** – Rev 19:16        
- **Leader of the armies of heaven** – implied in Rev 19:14        
- **Executor of God’s wrath** – Rev 19:15        
- **Victor over the beast, the kings of the earth, and their armies** – Rev 19:19–21

# Comparing descriptions to those of [[Gate of Wisdom/Beings/Jesus\|Jesus]]
- Title: Faithful and True (Rev 19:11)
	- Revelation 3:14 — Jesus is called “the faithful and true witness.”
- Judge and Warrior in Righteousness (Rev 19:11)
	- John 5:22–23 — The Father has given all judgment to the Son.
	- 2 Thessalonians 1:7–8 — Jesus executes vengeance on those who do not obey.
- King of Kings and Lord of Lords (Rev 19:16)
	- 1 Timothy 6:15 — Jesus is called “the blessed and only Potentate, the King of kings, and Lord of lords.”
- Leader of Heavenly Armies (Rev 19:14)
	- Joshua 5:13–15 — The “Commander of the Lord’s army” is recognized as divine, symbolically foreshadowing Christ.
	- 2 Thessalonians 3:3 — The Lord leads and protects His people.
- Executor of God’s Wrath (Rev 19:15)
	- Isaiah 63:3–4 — God treads the winepress of wrath; Christ shares in executing God’s justice.
	- Romans 12:19 — Vengeance belongs to God, often executed through Christ.
- Defeater of the Beast and Evil Forces (Rev 19:19–21)
	- 1 Corinthians 15:25–26 — Jesus must reign until all enemies are defeated, including death.
	- Psalm 2:9 — The Messiah will break the nations with a rod of iron.
